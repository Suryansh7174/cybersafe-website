About CyberSafe

CyberSafe is an initiative focused on raising awareness about cybersecurity in India. It covers phishing, digital identity protection, and cyber laws.

In this game, players navigate through cybersecurity challenges, learning how to spot scams, secure their passwords, and report threats.

Cybersecurity Tips

  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Beware of phishing emails and suspicious links.
  • Keep your software updated.
  • Use secure internet connections and avoid public Wi-Fi.
